TV Series

An App which completely changes the way used to track and manage your TV Shows

TV Series App allows you to track and manage your TV Shows without having to juggle between a desktop app to view your TV Shows and a separate app to track them. With TV Series you get to perform them just at the click of a button

Whats New in Version 2

  • Notification Center

    Notification Center keeps track of tasks performed by the App.

  • User Settings

    Manage your backup and Auto Update settings

  • Revamped Video Player Look

    A Full Screen Video Player with multi-touch gestures providing smooth transition between start of new episodes and for previous next episode commands. An Informtaion bar along with the Video to get quick information about the currently playing and the nearby episodes without having to leave the video

    To know more about the supported video formats visit Supported audio and video formats (Windows Runtime apps)

  • Redesigned Favorites Section

    Favorites Section has been redesigned providing you with series rating, interactive links to your last watched and coming Up episodes

Bug Fixes

  • Search TV Shows

    The Search is now stable and also keeps tracks of your already added TV Shows

  • Search Episodes

    Search Tool for finding episodes in a series is now more responsive to continuous keystrokes and does get stuck on continous searches
